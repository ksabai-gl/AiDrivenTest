# Funding liteERP

liteERP is an open-source, extension-first ERP platform built with a long-term
focus on maintainability, extensibility, and ownership of data.

The project is developed in the open and intended to remain independent,
vendor-neutral, and community-driven.

---

## Why we are asking for support

We are **not** raising funds to accelerate feature development or to commercialize
the project.

We are seeking **small, predictable sponsorships** to help cover **basic
infrastructure costs**, including:

- Demo / staging servers
- CI & test runners
- Documentation hosting
- Build and release automation

Keeping these services running ensures that contributors and users can evaluate,
test, and rely on the project over time.

---

## How much support is needed

liteERP does not require a large budget.

A modest amount (approximately **$50–$100 per month**) is sufficient to keep the
project infrastructure online and stable.

---

## Sponsorship principles

To maintain trust and independence, liteERP follows these principles:

- Sponsorship **does not** grant decision-making power
- Sponsorship **does not** influence the roadmap
- Sponsorship **does not** provide exclusive features
- The core will remain open-source

All technical decisions are guided by architectural integrity and community needs.

---

## Ways to support

If liteERP is useful to you or your organization, you may consider supporting the
project via:

- **GitHub Sponsors** (monthly or yearly)
- **Company sponsorship** to support infrastructure costs

Even small contributions help keep the project sustainable.

---

## Transparency

Infrastructure usage and sponsorship goals will be kept simple and transparent.
The intent is sustainability — not growth at all costs.

---

Thank you for supporting open, long-lived software.
